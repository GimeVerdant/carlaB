Your story

Healing begins where the wound was made.

"Healing begins where the wound was made" — Alice Walker

Telling your story can be an important part of therapy — reflecting on your past, your childhood, and the relational and emotional environments that have shaped you. We may also consider intergenerational patterns: the ways experiences, beliefs, and wounds can be carried across families, often outside of conscious awareness.

We can also work with where you are now, focusing on your current experience and where you would like to get to. Therapy can move fluidly between past and present, always guided by what feels most relevant and supportive for you.
